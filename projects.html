    Welcome to Fleet! After you log-in or sign-up, you are met with the option to create, join or view a trip. We will start off by creating one.
    Once you are navigated to the create screen there is a prompt to enter a trip name and unique code. This code is how other users will be able to join your trip.
    Next, enter your starting location and end location. One feature we implemented was auto-complete for the most relevant location typed in.
    To finalize the trip details, the creator of the trip is prompted to enter a gas price. This decesion was made after long consideration from our team, and we came to the conclusion that creators will determine the cost. To ensure full transparency, passengers of the trip are made aware of this ability and what the cost is.
    When you would like to join a trip as a driver, on the home screen you can select Join where you will be prompted to enter the trip code and check that you are a driver.
    Next, select a vehicle from a list associated to your account. When you add a vehicle to your account the details needed are a vehicle name, MPG and passenger seats
    While in a trip, you can view the trip details and all the passengers in your vehicle. You also have the ability to end a trip which will notify all passengers how much they owe given the distance traveled, gas price and MPG.
    Alternatively, if you were to join a trip as a passenger you would enter the trip code and simply leave the driver box unchecked then press Join trip
    When you join a trip as a passenger you are navigated to a trip information screen. On this screen you can view the start and end location as well as gas price. Also the trip code is displayed at the top so you can copy and share with friends! At the bottom you will press Vehicles to choose who you're riding with.
    After navigating to this screen, you can either view all the passengers in the group or select a vehicle.

    Similar to the driver's active trip screen, you will be able to view the trip details and all the passengers in your vehicle. The only difference is that you are not able to end the trip.
    After selecting Trips on the homescreen you will see a list of all the trips you have been on. Along with some details associated with each.
