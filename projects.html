    Fleet

    Junior Design - Spring 2023

    Our mission is to eliminate the time wasted organizing group travel as well as the uncertainty of how much a driver should be compensated. The story below will demonstrate exactly how we accomplish this task.

    Welcome to Fleet!
    After you log-in or sign-up, you are met
    with the option to create, join or view a trip.
    We will start off by creating one.

    Create Trip

    Once you are navigated to the create screen
    there is a prompt to enter a trip name and unique
    code. This code is how other users will be able
    to join your trip.

    Next, enter your starting location and
    end location. One feature we implemented was
    auto-complete for the most relevant location typed in.

    To finalize the trip details, the creator of the trip is
    prompted to enter a gas price. This decesion was
    made after long consideration from our team, and we came
    to the conclusion that creators will determine the cost.
    To ensure full transparency, passengers of the trip are
    made aware of this ability and what the cost is.

    Join as Driver

    When you would like to join a trip as a driver,
    on the home screen you can select Join where
    you will be prompted to enter the trip code and check
    that you are a driver.

    Next, select a vehicle from a list associated to your
    account. When you add a vehicle to your account the details
    needed are a vehicle name, MPG and passenger seats

    While in a trip, you can view the trip details and
    all the passengers in your vehicle. You also have
    the ability to end a trip which will notify all passengers
    how much they owe given the distance traveled,
    gas price and MPG.

    Join as Passenger

    Alternatively, if you were to join a trip as a passenger
    you would enter the trip code and simply leave the
    driver box unchecked then press Join trip

    When you join a trip as a passenger you are navigated to
    a trip information screen. On this screen you can
    view the start and end location as well as gas price.
    Also the trip code is displayed at the top so you can copy
    and share with friends!

    At the bottom you will press Vehicles to choose who you're riding with.

    After navigating to this screen, you can either view
    all the passengers in the group or select a vehicle.

    Similar to the driver's active trip screen, you
    will be able to view the trip details and
    all the passengers in your vehicle. The only
    difference is that you are not able to end the trip.

    Trip History

    After selecting Trips on the homescreen you
    will see a list of all the trips you have been on.
    Along with some details associated with each.

    The information saved in your history is the
    start and end location and gas price.
