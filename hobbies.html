    Guitar Fish

    • Date:

      07/01/2022

    • Method:

      Bottom rig baited with squid and 2oz weight

    • Description:

      The largest fish I had ever caught up to this point. While reeling in my line to recast, I felt an odd surplus of weight. Initially I thought nothing of it other than more seaweed to take off, but I began to feel the 'seaweed' fight back. After the help of another fisherman I was able to get this photo to cap off the beautiful sunset.
